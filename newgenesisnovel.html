New Genesis novel

This Novel is an ebook. It opens in your browser. You do not have to be online. Just make sure you extract all the files. Click on the main "index" file. The book will expand and you can read it like a real book.

 

The story is about a small group who survives the destruction of the world. Led by

a young African American scientist, they have to travel the length of the U.S. on foot

to acclimate their biological systems to a changed earth that no longer supports life as it used to be. They must keep ahead of the last and most deadly attack from the sun of all. An attack they had predicted to come within 7 years. All the time they had to reach a different Continent in a world where nothing mechanical worked and in which they had been thrown back into an age where all they had to protect themselves were the weapons of the most primitive of peoples.

 

This novel comes with a soundtrack of the same name. The soundtrack is free.

But you have to put  the mp3 files in the  "contents" folder of the book yourself because I cannot profit from the music.

 

If you don't have a browser that works, try any "html editor" to open the main "index" file. Just make sure you extract all the files from the zip folder first.

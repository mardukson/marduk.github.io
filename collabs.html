The Collaboration Page

This is the collaboration Page.

 

This is where you will be able to find work I've done with others.

 

All the songs on this page were written 100% by me.

 

But the arrangements, music and of course vocals belongs to a very talented

Artist named Stacy Robinson.

 

The collab between us did not work out. Nothing was signed.

 

I did  promise Stacy that I would not use any of the work we did in the collaboration in any way.

 

That about 6 months ago.

 

I intended to keep my word and then I found out that not only was work produced during the collab

being used, it was being sold with another collaborator.

 

This is well within her rights besides the fact that though most of the lyrics were changed, a few lines of mine were used.

 

Still, I didn't write the music, and this was one of the songs that were completely different from

the way I envisioned it musically, when I wrote it.

 

Most of the songs on this page are somewhat similar to how I would have done them—none of them are exact--and a couple are completely different from what I would have produced.

 

I'm not concerned that this song was used with no credit to me, but I do think that it releases me from any ethical concern about using the collabs as a showcase to other Artists.

 

I'm not going to do them to sell the way she did them. I'll do them my way or let the Artist who wants to do this album(I have more songs for it) do them her way.

 

If you want to do this album, just be able to sing and be ready to sign an agreement that we will draw up together.
